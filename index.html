Orange County Vajra Vidya 
(OCVV)
Newport Beach, California

Cape Ann Vajra Vidya is a Buddhist Meditation and Study Group meeting weekly, under the direction of the Venerable Khenchen Thrangu Rinpoche, an eminent meditation master and scholar of the Karma Kagyu lineage of Tibetan Buddhism. All are welcome!

When: Monday nights, 7:00 - 9:00 PM for meditation and discussion. 

Where: Sacred Heart Church Parish Hall, 62 School Street, Manchester-by-the-Sea, MA.  The Parish Hall is Handicap accessible; enter from the parking lot behind the church and use the lift to the main level.  (See below for directions)

What: Cape Ann Vajra Vidya Monday meetings begin with an hour of meditation (generally a combination of sitting and walking meditation) followed by an hour of discussion. Meditation instruction is free and always available. As a group we read and discusses Buddhist books and/or teaching videos. Study topics include tranquility and insight meditation, working with obstacles, and the development of mindfulness, awareness and compassion.

Cost: $7.00 weekly or $80 per quarter.  The first visit is always free. Attendance is not dependent on an ability to pay; please contribute what you can afford. Everyone is welcome.

Directions:We meet in the Parish Hall of Sacred Heart Church at 62 School Street, Manchester-by-the-Sea, MA.  The Parish Hall is accessible from the parking lot located behind the church, off Burnham Lane. After exiting your car, follow signs to “Tara Montessori School"

From Rte. 128 North or South:  Take Exit 15, School Street, Manchester and travel toward Manchester Center. Continue straight through the 4-way stop intersection at Pleasant St. In about .3 miles you will pass the Sacred Heart Church on your right. Immediately after passing the church, turn right onto Burnham Lane and then take another right into the church parking lot. The Parish Hall is a medium-size brick building with many windows at the farthest end of the parking lot. A short walkway from the parking lot will bring you to the rear of the building. Follow signs for “Tara Montessori School”. A motion-activated light is located on the building. Enter through the first door you come to, and ascend a short flight of stairs (6 or 7 steps) to the hall. An electric “lift” is available to assist those who need it. 

From Rte. 127, Manchester center:  Turn onto School Street at the intersection of Central and Union Streets, opposite Manchester Town Hall. Continue on School Street for .3 miles and turn left at Burnham Lane at the Sacred Heart Church. Follow directions to parking lot and Parish Hall as described above.
